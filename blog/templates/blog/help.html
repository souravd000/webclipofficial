How to change your password while you're logged in

1. Log in to your account and go to your profile by clicking Profile Button from the navigation bar.

2. Click on Settings button.

3. From the Pop up modal click on Reset Password.

4. Enter Your Email address which is associated with your account.

5. Click on Request Password Reset.

6. Check your email inbox. Webclip will immediately send a mail to your account's email address.

7. The email will include a link.

8. Click on the link, Which will direct you to a new page.

9. Type your New Password and type your same password in the Confirm New Password field.

10. Click on Reset Password button.

11. Your new password Has been set. Now you can login by clicking on login button.

How to change your password while you're logged out or in case you can't login or you forget your password

1. Go to login page, click Forget Password.

2. From there follow the steps (4 to 11) from the top.

If you have one email address associate with more than one Webclip account you will eventually get reset password mail for each webclip account in your associate email address.

How to Comment on a post

To Comment on a post, follow the steps below:

1. Click on the button from the bottom-right of the post.

2. You will be redirected to the post detail page.

3. Scroll down to bottom.

4. Write your texts with some emoji or comments in the Content field.

5. Click SUBMIT button.

The user of the post will get a notification if someone comments on their post. If you comment on your post you will receive that notification too. You can also deactivate comments for an individual post.

How to Repost

To Reposting a post, follow the steps below:

1. Click on the button on bottom-right of the post.

2. The page will redirect you to the home page.

By Reposting, your reposted content will be the same as the post content which you reposted. After reposting you can update your reposted content from your profile. You can also deactivate Reposting for an individual post. Users will get notified when you are reposting their content.

You can disable comments and repost for each post. Disabling comment and repost can be done when your uploading a post.

To disable comments and repost for each post, follow the steps below:

1. Click on button from navigation bar.

2. Select your photo and video and write your text content.

3. Click on Restrict comments and Restrict repost checkboxes to disable both Comment and Repost.

4. Click on Restrict comments check box to disable only comments.

5. Click on Restrict repost check box to disable only repost.

4. Click on post button.

You can go for only comments or repost, or you can disable both comments and repost.

On other people's posts or your posts, you can only delete comments that you've written. Deleting your comment will delete the replies associate with it.

To delete a comment and the replies within it, follow the steps below:

1. If you write a comment a Delete button will be there below the comment.

2. Click on Delete button.

3. You will be redirected to a new page where you have to confirm delete.

4. Press the yes, delete button.

Currently, there is no delete button for the reply, we are working on that feature. Deleting comments will delete the reply within it.
