Profile Verification

To verify your profile, follow the steps below:

1. Update your profile with your name, profile photo, Date of Birth, and Location. You have to make those changes before submitting the form.

2. Make sure Your Display Name, Date of birth, and location are as same as your Government Authorized Id information.

3. Make sure you change your profile picture with a current single photo of yours.

4. Don't change your information until your profile verification otherwise we will not be able to verify your profile.

5. Click on the link or the Verify button below and submit the form.

Note: We will shortly notify you. The process may take 3 to 4 days. After getting your verified profile badge or a blue tick, You can change your profile informations as your wish.
