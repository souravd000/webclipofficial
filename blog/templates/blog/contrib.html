How to apply for contribution account?
    • First, Select the edit profile button and go to the edit profile page.
    • You have to verify your account vefore proceeding to your contribution account. how to verify?
    • Update your profile with your bank information. we recommend you to use paypal account.
    • you can download your UPI QR code from your digital bank provider
    • you have to upload the picture of your QR code.
    • Under the QR code please provide your bank account number and the name of the bank.
    • Last, you have to press the update button for update your profile

    you can give any digital bank provider(including Paypal, paytm, phonepe, etc.) account name and information.
